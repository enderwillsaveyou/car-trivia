# Image Prompt: AMC AMX

Create a photorealistic mobile trivia game image of a classic American car: AMC AMX.

The image should show a front 3/4 angle focused on the hood, grille, and headlights. The car should be centered and recognizable by its body shape, hood lines, grille design, headlights, stance, and classic proportions.

Vehicle-specific direction:
late 1960s two-seat muscle car, compact aggressive stance, no visible emblems. Body color must be Big Bad Blue (vivid period-correct AMC blue) or Big Bad Green (vivid period-correct AMC green) — NOT copper, orange, red, or any warm metallic. Emphasize the AMX's defining two-seater proportions: short rear deck, truncated fastback roofline ending abruptly behind the doors, and NO rear quarter window — the roofline is clearly shorter and stubbier than a four-seat pony car. Long hood relative to the very short rear. The visual identity must read as distinctly different from the AMC Javelin, which is a longer four-seat fastback.

Style requirements:
- Photorealistic
- 1960s–1970s classic American car aesthetic
- Dramatic indoor garage/studio lighting
- Dark neutral background
- Soft reflections on paint and chrome
- Low camera angle, slightly above headlight level
- Hood and headlights should dominate the frame
- Front 3/4 view, not full side view
- Clean cinematic composition
- Sharp detail on headlights, grille, hood, bumper, and front fenders
- High-end automotive photography look
- Period-correct
- Mostly stock
- Believable, not over-customized

Restrictions:
- No people
- No visible logos
- No badges
- No brand emblems
- No license plates
- No readable text
- No decals
- No racing numbers
- No watermarks
- No modern cars
- No futuristic styling
- No cartoon style
- No illustration style
- No distorted proportions
- No extra cars in the background
- No copper or orange paint (reserved for the AMC Javelin in this set)
- No rear quarter windows

Aspect ratio:
16:9 landscape

Recommended image size:
1792x1024 or similar high-resolution landscape format.
