# Master Image Style Rules

Game image style:
All images must feel like they belong to the same mobile game asset set.

Use this global visual direction:
- Photorealistic
- 1960s-1970s classic American car aesthetic
- Front 3/4 angle
- Focus on hood, grille, headlights, bumper, and front fenders
- Car centered in frame
- Dramatic indoor garage/studio lighting
- Dark neutral background
- Soft reflections on paint and chrome
- Low camera angle, slightly above headlight level
- Clean cinematic automotive photography
- Same lighting, same camera feel, same background style across every image
- Period-correct body shape and proportions
- Mostly stock appearance
- Recognizable through silhouette, front-end design, hood lines, grille, headlights, and stance
- 16:9 landscape aspect ratio
- High resolution, ideally 1792x1024 or similar landscape size

Restrictions for every image:
- No visible logos
- No badges
- No brand emblems
- No license plates
- No readable text
- No decals
- No racing numbers
- No people
- No watermarks
- No extra cars
- No modern cars
- No futuristic styling
- No cartoon style
- No illustration style
- No distorted proportions
- Do not include the answer text in the image
