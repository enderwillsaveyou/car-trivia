# Image Prompt: Garage Backdrop

Create a gritty photorealistic classic American muscle-car garage backdrop for a mobile trivia game UI.

The image should be a reusable portrait-oriented app background, not a car photo. It should provide atmosphere while keeping the center dark and readable for app content.

Style requirements:
- Photorealistic dark indoor garage wall and floor
- Subtle checkered racing texture
- Worn metal and distressed paper edge details
- Faint red workshop light glow
- Vintage automotive atmosphere
- High-detail raster image
- Dark charcoal base with muted red accents and worn off-white texture
- Portrait mobile composition
- Darker center area for app content

Restrictions:
- No readable text
- No logos
- No brand marks
- No badges
- No license plates
- No vehicles
- No people
- No watermarks
- No cartoon style
- No illustration style
- No futuristic styling
